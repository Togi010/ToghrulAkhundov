Membership

The Rotaract Club of Birmingham is the premier young professional organization in Birmingham, Alabama, where up-and-coming civic leaders gather and work together to fulfill the Club’s motto of Learn, Socialize, and Serve. If you are a young professional in Birmingham who desires leadership development while improving your community and networking with others, we invite you to apply for membership.

Application Requirements

  • Be 30 years of age or younger
  • Attend one learn event (includes a lunch)
  • Attend one social event (includes a pop-up or monthly social)
  • Obtain one letter of endorsement from a current member of the Rotaract Club of Birmingham
  • Obtain one letter of endorsement from a current member of any Rotary Club (non-family member is strongly preferred)
  • Complete an online membership application

Applications will be considered once all items, including both endorsements, are received by the Membership Recruitment Director by the due date. The three application deadlines are October 1st, February 1st, and June 1st. For any questions about the application or

recruitment process, please email recruitment@birminghamrotaract.com.
